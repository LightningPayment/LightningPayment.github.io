Our Mission


We strive to create a world where people can make point of sale transactions without having to
fumble around with physical objects such as cash, cards and cell phones.


We are working towards two key goals:

1. Reduce the need for merchants to hand out physical change
and for consumers to have to carry loose change with them.

2. Eliminate the need for a traditional wallet or a phone, in
that anyone can make a transaction at any time using simply
their biometric identity.


Secure biometric-authorized payments are the future, and we want to make the future safe and
easy for you to operate. Eventually larger corporations may enter this space, but we want to
make it so that your privacy is the top priority, not profits.




What we do


We build biometric payment terminals that utilize facial and fingerprint recognition technology
to authorize transactions.

Using our terminals, people can opt to transfer any physical change that they receive on cash
transactions in the form of a cash balance. This balance can then be used on future transactions.

Most Importantly, our terminals allow people to link their preferred credit and debit cards as
well as any bank accounts to our online system during registration. In this way, people can make
transactions using any payment method on the go without carrying around any sort of wallet.
